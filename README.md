# Mathematical Thinking Games · Interactive

**Logic puzzles, combinatorics, and reasoning**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 1 · Foundation

## What You'll Learn

- Logic puzzles and truth tables
- Counting principles and combinatorics
- Pattern recognition and sequences
- Strategic thinking and game theory basics
- Mathematical proof by example

- 《数学思维训练》系列（华罗庚金杯辅导）

- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
